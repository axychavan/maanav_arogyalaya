What is Yog ?

Yoga is a comprehensive practice that encompasses physical, mental, and spiritual development. The practice of asanas, or physical postures, promotes physical health and wellness. Pranayama, or breath control, and chanting Omkar help to improve concentration, promote peace of mind, and increase positivity, leading to mental happiness. When the body and mind are healthy, it becomes easier to undertake spiritual practices, which is the ultimate goal of yoga. The goal of yoga is Samadhi, which is a state of self-realization, inner peace, and bliss. Through yoga practice, it is possible to stabilize the mind and become introspective, leading to spiritual progress. While many people may turn to yoga for physical healing, it is essential to remember that its true purpose is for spiritual upliftment, and it can become a meaningful lifestyle choice. Ultimately, practicing yoga can lead to lasting happiness, peace of mind, and spiritual progress, all of which are available to us for free.
