A Unique Way of Teaching

Madhusudanji has a few specific points that he likes to focus on while teaching his class -

Proper Exercise – Asana
Proper exercise acts as a method to make sure that all your muscles, joints, ligaments and tendons are flexible and have enough blood circulation.
Proper Breathing – Pranayama
This helps the body in becoming connected to its solar plexus, which is the core of the body. This also helps to increase your clarity and vitality.
Proper Relaxation – Savasana
Relaxation helps to give the body a reprieve and prevents it from becoming over-worked. Relaxation is necessary to have a healthy, functioning body.
Proper Diet – Ayurvedic diet
This helps our body get all nutrients, including sunlight and oxygen. All nutrients and fuel for our bodies are equally necessary for maintaining a healthy body.
Proper Thinking and Meditation – Vedanta and Dhyana
This helps you to put your mind in the correct place. This helps to settle your thoughts and lets you and your mind have a slight reprieve.
